TCS DIGITAL:

About:

        Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and 
        consulting company with its headquarters in Mumbai. It is a part of the Tata Group and operates in 149 
        locations across 46 countries. In July 2022, it was reported that TCS had crossed 6 lakh or 600,000 
        Employees worldwide.

Job Description for TCS Digital Hiring

        All TCS aspirants who would eventually be hired through TCS Digital exam would be groomed to work 
        on challenging digital engagements and trending technologies in areas like 
        Internet of Things, Big Data & Analytics, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, etc.
